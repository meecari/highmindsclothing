About Us

Founder CEO

Omar Baliw. A Pinoy hip-hop artist, well respected in the underground affiliated with ALC Fam, Tugmaan Records, Xdeal, Itskallab, and now his famous "High Minds Clothing" line. Entrepreneur and artist in one body.

He started way back in 2003 when some fusion of rap and band are a trend until he discovers and takes rap seriously, that we surely, that we surely take notice. Some collaborations and Personal mixtapes already in his resume as well as some rap battle. His "Tang Ina Song" track is the one that introduces me to him. The wordplays and dope lines of that song are still on our minds.

HIGH MINDS CLOTHING

High Minds Clothing is a brand that offers unique, beautifully designed clothing from around the Philippines. It is an independent streetwear brand that aims to uplift and inspire the masses.

With their individual styles and stories, the pieces they have to offer are sure to have something for everyone. Their goal is to inspire and educate through design and fashion: they carry everything from streetwear attire to mid-fashion, and everything in between.
